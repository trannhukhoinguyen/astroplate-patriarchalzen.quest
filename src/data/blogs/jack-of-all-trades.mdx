Hi there👋, this blog is going to tell the story of how being a jack of all trades can help you. Hope you'll enjoy it!

<H2>Story</H2>

There is a 🚢 ship named Sunny, sailed by a captain called Franky. The ship has seen many adventures and traveled across the earth 🌍

![Franky](/blog/jack-of-all-trades/franky.avif)

During one journey, the ship's ⚙️ engine got damaged. The ship needed repairs, so Franky planned to stop at the next island 🏝️ and search for a mechanic.

They ⚓ boarded the ship at the island and started searching for a mechanic.

![Boarded the ship](/blog/jack-of-all-trades/board-ship.avif)

They asked the 👥 villagers and found that a mechanic called Vegapunk lived on the southern side of the island.

Captain Franky and the villagers went in search of Vegapunk, and they finally found him!

![Vegapunk](/blog/jack-of-all-trades/vegapunk.avif)

He was quite old and walked very slowly. Franky explained the situation with their 🚢 ship, and together they went to fix it.

The old man carefully checked the entire ship for a long time. Then, he took a hammer and slammed it against the engine.

![Fixing the ship](/blog/jack-of-all-trades/fixing-ship.avif)

The engine started working like magic💫! Everyone was surprised by how quickly Vegapunk fixed the ship.

Vegapunk then gave Franky a bill for a whopping 1 million dollars🤯. Everyone was shocked after seeing the bill💵.

<H2>Takeaways</H2>

Being a pro at one thing matters a lot because it allows you to create great stuff around it. That’s why Vegapunk charged 1 million dollars.

> I fear not the man who has practiced 10,000 kicks once, but I fear the man who has practiced one kick 10,000 times - Brucelee

But that doesn’t mean ignoring everything else. Having knowledge of the things around you gives you a bigger picture of the situation and opens doors to new possibilities!
